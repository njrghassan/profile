Lilya’s Peace in Deep Pictures

Ghassan Alnajjar
May 6, 2024




Inroduction

I’ve never met anyone caring and passionate about their goals, but that changed when I met Lilya Oleshchinskaia, a dedicated student pursuing Nursing at Mount Royal University on her way to her third year. Lilya’s motivation for helping babies and children started when on the day of her sister's birth which was a big part of why she decided to choose Nursing and her passion for helping others.

Additionally, her caring about her family is an unbreakable kindness and highlights how worried is she about them because they have issues gathering, and that’s due to the conflict between Russia and Ukraine which lead to unreliable conditions and lack of flights on these areas because they are considered as dangerous areas by the EASA (Xu, 2023). However, she will be seeing her parents in Turkey, since they have good relationships with most countries (Wieting & Fraser, 2024), and as Lilya says, “God and then Family are the most important, so don’t let life get in the way”.


One Interesting Factoid

An interesting fact about Lilya is that photography is her favorite hobby, and she is very talented in it as she always takes pictures anywhere of anything she finds interesting. I find this fascinating because her photos talk by themselves and have a sense of peace and beauty in them.


Behind the Scene

During my conversation with Lilya, we were located at the campus of our university on the roof of the building where it was cloudy. The cloud gave me the perfect lighting as it was very bright and not dim, so getting the right angle to match the lighting wasn’t hard at all since everywhere had the same level of brightness.

Behind the bench, there were still growing bushes and leaves was the perfect background with the building behind to show that it’s an institution place and a bit of blur to highlight focus on the Lilya as she is the main story of the image. Lilya’s pose highlighted that she is a part of the place and that she is confident in herself. The smile gave more peacefulness to the image and added more to her personality.

Finally, the camera angle was a straightforward shot focusing directly on her, but there was a slight dent in the angle which resulted left side to be more higher which happened accidentally.
